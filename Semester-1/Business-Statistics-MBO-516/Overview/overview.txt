After completing this course, you should be able to:
-------------------------------------------------------
select appropriate statistical techniques for practical data analysis
analyse and interpret the results obtained from a range of statistical techniques
use the output from statistical analysis to aid in complex business decisions in today's highly globalised world
present the results of data analyses and explorations to a professional audience that might include policymakers and global business leaders
make decisions based on statistical analyses in a given work situation as you take up the role of a senior manager in an organisation.
